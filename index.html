A dock for your browser tabs.

Tab Dock groups your open tabs by website into a macOS-style dock. Available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Brave.

Everything happens in your browser. Tabs haven’t kept up.

Email, meetings, YouTube, Netflix, shopping — it all lives in tabs. And yet you still lose the Google Meet tab or the article you set aside. Working without a dock would be chaos on your desktop. Your browser deserves one too.

Features

Everything you need to tame your tabs. Nothing you don’t.

⌘

Dynamic Tab Dock

All tabs from the same web app are grouped at the bottom of your window, one icon per site.

→

Easy navigation

Click any tab in the dock to switch instantly — review what’s open and close what you don’t need.

~

Smooth animations

Delightful, fluid motion. The web doesn’t have to be rigid.

</>

Open source

All the code is public. Bring your ideas and improvements.

●

No tracking

Everything stays on your computer. Nothing leaves your browser.

How it works

1

Hover to expand

Move your mouse to the bottom of the window to reveal the dock.

2

Group and navigate

Each icon represents a web application. Hover to see its open tabs, click to switch.

3

Seamless integration

The dock is injected into every site you visit, for a consistent, integrated experience.
